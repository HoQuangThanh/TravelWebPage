Angkor Wat, Cambodia

Explore the ancient wonders of the Khmer Empire.

The Majestic Angkor Wat

Angkor Wat is a UNESCO World Heritage site and the largest religious monument in the world. This stunning temple complex, built in the 12th century, is a masterpiece of Khmer architecture and a symbol of Cambodia. Witness the intricate bas-reliefs, towering spires, and serene moats, especially beautiful during sunrise and sunset.

Beyond Angkor Wat: Bayon Temple

Just a short distance from Angkor Wat lies Bayon Temple, a unique and awe-inspiring structure within Angkor Thom. Famous for its multitude of serene, smiling stone faces, Bayon offers a captivating and mystical experience. Each face, carved into colossal towers, is believed to represent Avalokiteshvara, the bodhisattva of compassion, or King Jayavarman VII himself.
